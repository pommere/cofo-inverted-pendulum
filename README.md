# cofo-inverted-pendulum
The structure and configuration of the human leg allows it to behave like an inverted pendulum with relatively little muscle force. A relatively straight leg can support body weight with very little knee torque. [Ki-netic energy, gravitational potential energy], . . . work, and force together explain the advantage of the inverted pendulum [model].
